About Tonshive

Tonshive is a Stay-To-Earn hidden advertising serverice . Helps hoster makes more profite from customer page stays . Allows merchant to make protife from the user's page stays during minigame/miniapp , by using user's mobile phone computing power on randomX for XMR or other crypto mining .

Stay To Earn

Stay-To-Earn (STE) is a new way for defi builder or gamefi builder to construct their Dapp . It can be a part of Play-To-Earn model and allows the player to got more reward during the page stay .

$tXMR

$tXMR is the wrap XMR jetton on TON chains . $tXMR is fully back and withdrawable 1:1 to XMR .

Safe mining control

There will be only one mining process in one time , and the best way to use it is to settile the process rate less than 50% . It can avoid the overheat or process overload happened in customer device .
